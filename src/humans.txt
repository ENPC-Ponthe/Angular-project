/ * SITE * /
Galeries Ponthé is the photo and video gallery of Le Ponthé, Ecole des Ponts ParisTech's audiovisual club.
It gathers all the visual memories of students during their years at Ecole des Ponts.
Originally developed entirely in Python using Flask, this website was then completed with an Angular front-end.

Languages: French, English, Spanish
Technologies: Python / Flask, Angular
